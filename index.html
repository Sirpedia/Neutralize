NEUTRALIZE Official Guide


Neutralize is a Minecraft map similar to regular survival except you can pick from a few abilities upon join/death.


Now here's the twist...

Due to someone millions of years ago, EXP is nearly Unobtainable. It was seen as a threat to humanity, as upon gaining or losing levels, you would lose your abilities.

Mobs do not drop EXP. Enchanting is disabled. Sculk mining is HEAVILY ADVISED AGAINST!


Zombies had a mutation thousands of years ago which caused them to have unmatched strength..


Abilities

There are 4 main abilities you could wield, along with many custom items.


♠ Moneyplanter ♣

The moneyplanter is for those who desire wealth. Upon coming in contact with an Oak tree, the wood will turn into emerald blocks.


⚡ Mineralist ⚠

The mineralist is for those who value efficiency and haste. Upon stepping on an ore, the said ore(s) turn into their block form.


☁ Wind Surfer ✈

The wind surfer is for those who are in a rush and live in the skies. This ability grants you permanent Jump Boost IV and allows you to fly by holding a feather.


☢ Lavaman ♨

The lavaman is for those who have too much to live for and prioritize their safety. Like frost walker, upon walking on lava, blackstone appears under your feet to save you from lava.
